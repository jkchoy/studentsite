Interface Design

The Scope

Client: College

Project Type: Web Design (UI)

Compentencies: Typography, Photography, Layout

Software: Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

The Challenge

My task was to choose a multipage website and identify areas that needed improvement, including layout, navigation, and overall user experience. From there, I created a multipage wireframe and mockups to guide the redesign, focusing on a clean, modern look that aligned with the brand's goals.

The Solution

I started by reviewing the original website to pinpoint areas that could be improved in terms of layout, navigation, and overall visual appeal. Using style tiles and wireframes, I planned a refreshed design that felt modern and aligned with the brand's identity.The final result is a responsive, accessible website that stays true to the original design vision while offering a cleaner, more user-friendly experience from start to finish.

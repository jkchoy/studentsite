Motion Graphic

The Scope

Client: College

Project Type: Motion Graphics

Software: After Effects, Illustrator

Compentencies: Animation, Visual Style

The Challenge

To grasp the concept of motion graphics, it's helpful to understand how animating a bouncing ball incorporates fundamental animation principles. This task involved creating a "story" using a bouncing ball, ensuring the inclusion of at least five of the twelve principles of animation. The ball must exhibit realistic bouncing behavior, adhering to the rules of physics. We have the option to depict the motion within a single still frame or across multiple frames, be as simple or complex as we please. Bonus points were awarded if the ball returns to its original starting point, demonstrating a complete and cohesive animation loop.

The Solution

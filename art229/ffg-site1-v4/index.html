New to Pole?

Start your pole journey!

If you have ever thought about trying pole dancing but not sure where to start, you are not alone! Pole dancing is often mistakenly seen as only for the experienced or highly fit, but in reality, it is for everyoneâ€”whether you're new to fitness or seeking a fresh challenge.


Why Pole Dance for Fitness?

Pole dancing is more than just spinning and climbingâ€”it is a full-body workout that enhances strength, flexibility, and coordination. The best part is its scalability, allowing you to adjust the intensity to your fitness level. Whether you're looking to build core strength, improve posture, or increase flexibility, pole dancing can target various muscle groups while providing an engaging, dynamic workout. The combination of aerobic movement, strength training, and flexibility exercises helps to tone and sculpt your body, making it the perfect effective and fun way to stay fit.


A Supportive Community for All

Whether you are adding pole dancing to your fitness routine or just exploring something new, you'll be part of a supportive community. Judgement will not be tolerated. Though starting something new can feel intimidating, you'll quickly find yourself surrounded by others eager to explore this empowering and fun fitness form.


Preparation for Class

Wear comfortable, stretchy workout clothes that make you feel confident. Avoid wearing lotion or body oil, as it can make the pole slippery, along with removing rings and bracelets to prevent scratches. Bring an exercise mat and water.

The focus is on honoring the Goddess within, not nudity. While you can peel away layers in a sensual manner, the class encourages wearing shorts and a sports bra, tank top for comfort and group safety.
